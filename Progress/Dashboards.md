# 📊 Olist E-Commerce — Sales & Customer Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard built on real Brazilian e-commerce data from Olist, covering over 96,000 delivered orders across 2016–2018. The dashboard is designed to give business teams a clear, actionable view of sales performance, customer behavior, and delivery operations — without needing to touch a single line of code.

## 🔍 What This Dashboard Answers

**For Sales Teams:**
- Which product categories are driving the most revenue?
- What days of the week (Compared To Last Year ) generate the most sales?
- How does this year's revenue compare to last year, day by day?
- Which payment methods do customers prefer?

**For Operations Teams:**
- Which states have the highest late delivery rates?
- How long does delivery take on average across different regions?
- Is late delivery hurting customer satisfaction scores?

**For Customer & Marketing Teams:**
- What percentage of customers come back after their first purchase?
- Who are our most valuable customers?
- How are customers segmented by spending behavior?
- What Are the Most brazilian States That have Most Customers ?

## 📋 Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 — Sales Overview
A high level view of overall business performance.

- **Total Revenue** — overall revenue with year over year trend
- **Total Orders** — order volume with monthly sparkline
- **Revenue by Product Category** — which categories drive the business
- **Revenue by Weekday** — this year vs last year side by side comparison
- **Payment Method Breakdown** — how customers prefer to pay

---

### Page 2 — Delivery & Customer Experience
A deep dive into logistics performance and its impact on satisfaction.

- **On Time vs Late Orders** — split of delivered orders by punctuality
- **Average Delivery Days by State** — regional delivery speed comparison
- **Late Delivery Rate by State** — which regions need logistics attention
- **Review Score by Delivery Status** — the direct impact of late delivery on ratings

---

### Page 3 — Customer Segmentation
Understanding who the customers are and how they behave.

- **Customer Segments** — Champions, Loyal, Recent, At Risk, Lost
- **Average Spend by Segment** — revenue contribution of each group
- **Repeat vs One-time Customers** — retention overview
- **Top Sellers by Revenue and Rating** — marketplace seller performance

---

## 💡 Key Business Findings

1. **Health & beauty** is the top revenue category, driven by high order volume rather than high price points
2. **Late deliveries score 1.72 points lower** on average — the strongest driver of poor customer satisfaction
3. **Northern states face the worst delivery performance** — Alagoas has a late rate nearly 4x higher than São Paulo
4. **97% of customers purchase only once** — retention is the biggest commercial challenge for the marketplace
5. **Credit card dominates** at 74% of transactions, with customers averaging 3.5 installments per order
6. **Monday and Tuesday are peak trading days** — weekends consistently underperform
7. **Champions (87 customers) spend 5.6x more** than the average customer despite being a tiny group
